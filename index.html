Sobre a Loja

Welcome to World Music, the music house that will enchant your ears and inspire your soul! Located in the heart of the city, we are a unique destination for all music lovers, from experienced musicians to enthusiasts who are just beginning to explore this wonderful sonic universe. At World Music, we are committed to providing an exceptional experience to all our customers. Our wide selection of high-quality musical instruments spans everything from electric guitars and grand pianos to professional drums and renowned saxophones. We work only with the best brands in the market, ensuring the excellence and durability of the products we offer.
